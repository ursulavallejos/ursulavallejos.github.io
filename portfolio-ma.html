Modo Adulta

Description

Modo Adulta is an influencer that helps people to reach their best potential in finances, organization and their adulthood journey. As we grow and become adults life gets hard because we have lots of new responsabilities: María Victoria share all her tips, dos and don'ts and knowledge at "etiquette". With that in mind I am creating a habits tracker to her users so they can improve their levels on organization, finances, tasks to do at home, and more.
