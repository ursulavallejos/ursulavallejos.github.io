Switch

Description

Switch is a platform where people can "Switch" clothes with Stars coins. It is sustainable, it helps people closet and our enviroment. ¿How to get stars? User uploads a photo of a garment and obtain stars, then the user can get other garment that also has an specific amount of stars other user upload.

Design system

Switch is made with a user centered design. The team made interviews, forms and prototyping to get to know the user persona, so the app is perfect for them. Interactions, branding, design thinking and functionalities are created to help the user, named Carla, upload and get clothes in a safe, intuitive and useful way.
